About Me

I'm currently a student at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona pursuing a degree in computer information systems with an emphasis on Application Development. During my time, I've had the opportunity as an intern at MakeIT-3D, Inc. During my internship, I was in charge of several in-house 3D printing machines, providing maintenance, printing services, and product manufacturing.


My classmates, co-workers, and employers have described me as a hardworking leader who's passionate, determined, and a cooperative teammate. I've had a lot of experience in leadership positions as a shift leader and assistant manager. I've been apart of a number of teams over the past 3 years and currently continuing to work with more.


I'll be graduating in June 2021, and I plan to pursue a career as an application and web developer in the Los Angeles area. I'm excited to pursue a position in which I can help a company achieve its business growth and development. In the meanwhile, I continue to acquire new skills and build a professional network at Cal Poly Pomona by associating myself with Cal Poly community and clubs such as, MISSA. Please feel free to contact me if you would like to connect, or are aware of any potential opportunities for which you think I may be appropriate.
